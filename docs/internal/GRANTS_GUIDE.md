## Tier 1: High Probability

### 1.1 SR&ED Tax Credits (Federal + Quebec)

**Scientific Research & Experimental Development**

The largest source of R&D funding in Canada. Provides tax credits for eligible R&D work.

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Federal Rate (CCPC)** | 35% refundable on first $3M |
| **Federal Rate (Standard)** | 15% non-refundable |
| **Quebec Rate** | 14-30% depending on company size |
| **Combined Maximum** | Up to 65% of eligible R&D costs |
| **Filing Deadline** | 18 months from tax year end |

**Eligible Activities for Forge Protocol:**
- Self-healing protocol development (ADR-002, ADR-003)
- Ethics protocol design (ADR-008)
- Anti-sycophancy research (ADR-015)
- Velocity measurement methodology (ADR-010)

**Links:**
- [SR&ED Program Overview](https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/scientific-research-experimental-development-tax-incentive-program.html)
- [SR&ED Eligibility](https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/scientific-research-experimental-development-tax-incentive-program/sred-eligibility.html)
- [HelloDarwin SR&ED Guide 2025](https://hellodarwin.com/business-aid/programs/sred)

**Canada's premier innovation funding program for SMEs.**

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Funding Amount** | Up to $10M (typical: $50K-$500K) |
| **Coverage** | 60-80% of eligible R&D costs |
| **Duration** | Up to 24 months |
| **Eligibility** | Canadian SME, <500 employees, incorporated |

**Program Components:**

| Small Tech Innovation | Up to $50K | Accelerated R&D projects |
| Mid-sized Projects | Up to $500K | Larger R&D initiatives |

**Why Forge Protocol Qualifies:**
- Novel AI technology with no prior art
- Documented technological uncertainties
- Commercial potential (published on crates.io)
